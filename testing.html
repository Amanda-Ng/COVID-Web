NYC: Free COVID-19 Testing and Anti-Viral Pills Available

Free COVID-19 testing and anti-viral pills are available at Test & Treat sites across the city. The mobile unites provide instant access to anti-viral medication (Paxolvid) for eligible citizens who test positive at the sites.

The medication is in pill form and is prescribed by a healthcare provider. Each Test & Treat site will be equipped with a clinician who can screen and prescribe the anti-viral pill for anyone who tests positive at the site.
