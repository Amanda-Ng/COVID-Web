What is COVID-19?

Coronavirus refers to the family of viruses that can cause symptoms including, but not limted to, severe acute respiratory syndrome (MERS), the common cold, and Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS). Known as severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-COV-2), this disease is more commonly called COVID-19. In March of 2020, the World Heath Organization (WHO) officialy declared the COVID-19 outbreak a pandemic.
